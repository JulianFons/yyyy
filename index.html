Hey there!

I am thrilled to introduce myself as a Civil Engineer from the Universidad Industrial de Santander in Bucaramanga, Colombia. With an exciting seven-year journey, I have parcipated in diverse projects, including dams, bridges, tunnels, buildings and PV and wind towers. Complemented by a Master's in Infrastructure Planning, I approach projects holistically, considering economic, environmental, and social implications.

My academic journey has led me through inspiring research institutes and dynamic companies, allowing me to blend theory with real-world dynamism. I have accumulated academic experiences in research institutes and companies, bringing my expertise to various domains. I pride myself on being a productive and dedicated individual, committed to delivering high-quality results in every project I undertake.

Institute of Energy Economics and Rational Energy Use (IER)

Formulate diverse analysis scenarios about advancing sustainable energy land use outcomes up to 2030 in Baden-Wüttemberg, Germany.
Additionally, considering energy efficiency, power and heat supply and demand, energy infrastructure, and agricultural and forestry land utilization, a low/zero land use strategy is developed using Geographic Information Systems (GIS).

Hydroelectrical Project Plant
(ITUANGO)

Oversee the technical data about the Ituango hydroelectric project Antioquia, Colombia; encompassing geotechnical, hydrological, seismic, and geodetic information, to effectively address the mandates set forth by governmental entities.

TUM-BORIG Innovation in Tunnel

Part of the no-profit student initiative from the Technical University of Munich (TUM), which won the Not-a-Boring Competition in 2023 in TEXAS, USA. An international event for tunnel technology organized by Elon Musk (Not-a-Boring Competition).
